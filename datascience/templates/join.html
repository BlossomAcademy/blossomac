At Blossom Academy, we give you the analytical and leadership skills required to launch a meaningful career in data science. Our Fast Track program is focused on Introduction to Machine Learning.

Our Promise

Acquire the complex skills of a 21st century business strategist by working through a series of realistic projects. You will learn how to apply methods of data analytics to predict buying trends, learn about data mining using R and Python, develop advanced visualization techniques to make your data sets both intuitive and beautiful, and many more. You will also build and deliver convincing presentations to "sell" your ideas. This training program is also supplemented by a series of guest lectures, featuring the experiences and insights of internationally-recognized executives and successful entrepreneurs.

Competitive Peer Learning Mentor-Based Learning Project-Based Learning
You will be placed in small groups to work together as a team and complete academic goals; which has goals at the individual and group level. These groups are formerly known as ‘Blossomists.’
A mentor will be available at all times during office hours to provide help and evaluate your work. We offer you a supportive and engaging work environment, where you can feel free to make mistakes and learn from your experiences.
Our learning methodology focuses 100% on “project-based learning.” You will work on projects similar to those you’ll find on the job and complete them using the same tools used by professionals currently in data science positions.
