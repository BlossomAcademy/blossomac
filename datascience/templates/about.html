We believe there are massive pools of incredibly talented youth across Africa; it is imperative to connect them to the global economy.

We are on a movement to build the next generation of Ghanaian data scientists

Technology startups are generating large amounts of data about their customers and their buying behavior. However, they cannot find nearly enough data scientists to analyze this data for strategic decision making.

According to IBM, the number of data science roles is projected to see a demand spike by 28% and job listings are projected to grow to approximately 2.7 million by 2020. Africa, meanwhile, has the youngest population and 7 of the 10 fastest growing internet populations in the world.

We founded Blossom Academy in 2018 to create a pathway where Ghanaian university graduates with diverse backgrounds can build the knowledge, skills and abilities to reach their potential as data science professionals and leverage those talents to contribute to the growth and success of our clients.
