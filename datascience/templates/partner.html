Our Selection Process

1. Rigorous aptitude tests

Our selection process starts with an aptitude test in collaboration with IBM Big Data University to assess data aptitude.

2. Technical case studies

The top 10% of candidates are invited to work on two case studies, with one being technical to assess grit.

3. Programming evaluations

The top 10% of candidates from the technical case studies are invited to take self-paced programming courses to assess their programming expertise.

4. In-Person interviews

Our in-person interviews, which mainly focuses on leadership skills and professional etiquette, guides us in recruiting our initial cohort for training. Overall, we select about 0.7% of applicants.
